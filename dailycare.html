🧘‍♂️ Daily Routine for Heart & Brain Health

1️⃣ Morning Routine (6:00 AM – 9:00 AM)

  • Wake up early and drink 1–2 glasses of water to activate your body.
  • Do 30 minutes of brisk walking or light jogging.
  • Try deep breathing or meditation for 10 minutes to reduce stress.
  • Eat a healthy breakfast — include oats, fruits, eggs, or nuts.

2️⃣ Midday Routine (12:00 PM – 2:00 PM)

  • Eat a balanced lunch — vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  • Drink at least 8–10 glasses of water per day.
  • Take a 10–15 minute walk after lunch to help blood circulation.

3️⃣ Evening Routine (5:00 PM – 8:00 PM)

  • Do 20–30 minutes of light exercise — yoga, cycling, or stretching.
  • Avoid heavy snacks; choose fruits or green tea instead.
  • Spend time relaxing — read, listen to music, or talk with family.

4️⃣ Night Routine (9:00 PM – 10:30 PM)

  • Eat a light dinner — soups or salads are good choices.
  • Turn off screens 30 minutes before bed to reduce stress.
  • Sleep for at least 7–8 hours daily for full body recovery.

🧠 Weekly Care Tips

  • Check your blood pressure and sugar levels twice a week.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol intake.
  • Include foods rich in Omega-3, magnesium, and antioxidants.
  • Manage stress through yoga, meditation, or outdoor walks.
Stay consistent — small daily habits protect your heart and brain ❤️
