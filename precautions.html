Daily Precautions

  • Exercise 30 minutes a day 🏃
  • Eat low-salt, low-fat, high-fiber food 🥗
  • Drink enough water 💧
  • Avoid smoking & alcohol 🚭
  • Manage stress and get enough sleep 🧘‍♂️
  • Check BP and sugar regularly
Good habits protect your heart & brain ❤️
