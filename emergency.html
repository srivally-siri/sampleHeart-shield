🚑 What To Do During a Stroke

Use the FAST method:

  • Face drooping
  • Arm weakness
  • Speech problems
  • Time to call emergency services!

Emergency Numbers:

  • 🚨 Ambulance (India): 108
  • 🏥 Global Emergency: 911
